Various Traits

Western signs

Aries

A born leader. A fearless and brave mind. Rather free-spirited. Impulsive and assertive nature.

Taurus

Persistent, tenacious and patient. Calm and trustworthy personality. Possibly stubborn.

Gemini

Enthusiasm and friendly nature. Gifted in communication.A flexible mind although superficial.

Cancer

A sensitive person with plenty of compassion. Very organized. Aggressive attitude in certain conditions.

Leo

Ambitious and confident. Generous. A leading and often dominating attitude.

Virgo

Very good problem solver, thanks to a logical and analytical mind. Maybe looking for too much attention.

Libra

Diplomatic attitude and easy-going nature. A taste for harmony and beauty. Indecisive when facing choices.

Scorpio

Passionate and emotional by nature. Resourceful and mentally strong. Vindictive when cheated.

Sagittarius

Open-minded to novelty, curious and even adventurous. Versatile nature also.

Capricorn

Responsible and highly motivated. Patience for reaching goals. A bit too cynical sometimes.

Aquarius

Altruistic and independent. Inventive. Emotional detachment could be interpreted as selfishness.

Pisces

Very creative and intuitive. Kind and compassionate. However not always accessible.

Vedic signs

Mesha

Fearless and courageous. Frankness and open-mindedness. Stubborn at times.

Vrishba

Lover of music. Pretty silent. Rather conservative and materialistic.

Mithuna

Changeable, adaptable. Good orator. Fast at work. Short-tempered but easily cooled down.

Karka

Mostly introvert. Sensitive, sympathetic. Ambitious, autocratic and strong-willed.

Simha

Bright, courageous, ambitious, proud. Easily relating to all kind of people. At times ill-tempered.

Kanyaa

Sensitive, shy. Practical and observant. Able to fix and adapt. Maybe to much orderliness.

Tula

Easily mixing with people. Imaginative and entertaining. A tendency to procrastinate.

Vrischika

Honest, sincere, trustworthy. Courageous and self-confident. Determined to face challenges head-on.

Dhanu

Truthful and generous. Loyal but independent. Impulsiveness is the greatest weakness.

Makara

A practical goal-oriented mindset. Confident with a taste for challenges. Too interested with gain at times.

Kumbha

Emotional, intuitive and imaginative. Human and compassionate. Social is more important than personal.

Meena

Courageous and timid. Popular, easygoing nature and tolerant. Emotional rather than rational.
